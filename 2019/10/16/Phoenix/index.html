  • UNSIGNED_LONG

  • SMALLINT
  • UNSIGNED_SMALLINT
  • FLOAT

  • DOUBLE
  • UNSIGNED_DOUBLE
  • DECIMAL
  • BOOLEAN
  • TIME
  • DATE
  • TIMESTAMP
  • UNSIGNED_TIME
  • UNSIGNED_DATE
  • UNSIGNED_TIMESTAMP
  • VARCHAR
